## How to Listen on Alexa (Amazon Echo)

Alexa devices like the Echo Dot and Echo Studio make streaming radio effortless, but you need to enable the skill first for the best experience.

### Step 1: Enable the TalesFM Skill

Before you speak, ensure Alexa knows where to find the station.

* Open the Alexa App on your phone.
* Go to More > Skills & Games.
* Search for "TalesFM".
* Tap Enable to Use.

### Step 2: Use Voice Commands

Once enabled, you can control TalesFM directly. Try these specific commands:

* "Alexa, open TalesFM." (Starts the service)
* "Alexa, ask TalesFM to play Jazz."
* "Alexa, ask TalesFM for news stations from London."
* "Alexa, ask TalesFM to play 80s hits."

### Step 3: Manage Playback

Control your session hands-free:

* "Alexa, pause."
* "Alexa, next station."
* "Alexa, set volume to five."

Pro Tip: If Alexa has trouble understanding the station name, create a Routine in the Alexa App. Set it so that when you say "Alexa, Morning Radio," it executes the command "Open TalesFM."

## How to Listen on Google Home (Nest Audio)

Google Home and Nest Audio deliver simplicity and smart integration. You can use voice commands or "Cast" audio directly from your phone.

### Option A: Voice Commands

Ensure your Google Home is connected to Wi-Fi.

* Say: "Hey Google, talk to TalesFM."
* Once connected, say:
* "Play classic rock."
* "Play global news."

### Option B: Google Cast (Best for Specific Stations)

If you are browsing TalesFM on your phone (Android or Chrome browser):

1. Open https\://www\.google.com/search?q=TalesFM.com on your device.
2. Start playing a station.
3. Tap the Cast icon on your player or browser menu.
4. Select your Google Home/Nest Speaker.
5. The audio will seamlessly transfer to the speaker in high quality.

### The Universal Bluetooth Connection (Works on All Devices)

If you don't want to set up skills or if your internet connection is spotty, Bluetooth is the fastest backup option.

For Alexa:

* Say: "Alexa, connect Bluetooth."
* Go to your phone’s Bluetooth settings and pair with "Echo."
* Open https\://www\.google.com/search?q=TalesFM.com on your phone browser and hit play.

For Google Home:

* Say: "Hey Google, turn on Bluetooth."
* Pair your phone with "Nest Audio" or "Google Home."
* Stream any station from TalesFM directly from your mobile.

## Alexa vs Google Home: Which is Better for Online Radio?

When choosing between Amazon Alexa (Echo) and Google Home (Nest) for online radio, the decision depends on your preferred listening style. 

Alexa excels in a purely voice-controlled environment; once you enable the TalesFM skill, it offers a robust hands-free experience and generally superior audio quality on high-end devices like the Echo Studio.

Google Home, however, offers better integration with your smartphone. While it supports voice commands via "Talk to" actions, its standout feature is the built-in Chromecast, allowing you to cast audio directly from your phone’s browser in higher quality than standard Bluetooth. 

Ultimately, both platforms support TalesFM seamlessly. Choose Alexa if you want a "set it and forget it" voice skill experience, or choose Google Home if you prefer the flexibility of casting from your mobile device.
