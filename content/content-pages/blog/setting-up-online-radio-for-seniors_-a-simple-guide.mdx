## Step-by-Step: How to Set Up Online Radio for Seniors

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to set up the online radio for seniors:

1\. Choose the Device They Prefer

TalesFm works beautifully on:

Smartphones: Android & iPhone

Tablets: Bigger screens make navigation easier

Laptops: Ideal for at-home listening

Smart Speakers: Alexa / Google Home

Pick whatever they’re comfortable with. Bigger screens often make things easier.

2\. Open TalesFm in the Browser

No installs. No confusion.

Just open the browser and type in:

talesfm.com

Tip: Add it to the home screen so it becomes a single-tap shortcut or download the app from playstore or apple store.

3\. Show Them How to Search for a Station

Use the search bar to find:

Old Bollywood radio

Bhakti / devotional stations

News channels

Retro English

Regional stations

International radio

Keep it to 2–3 favorite stations so it’s easy for them to remember.

4\. Create a “Favorites” List

Tell them:

“Whenever a station feels right, press the heart to store it in your favorites.”

This creates a custom library of stations they can replay anytime without searching.

5\. Adjust Volume and Playback Settings

Keep volume buttons easily reachable

Increase device font size to make buttons easier

Enable “Do Not Disturb” to avoid random notifications interrupting their listening

6\. For Smart Speakers 

If they prefer voice commands:

Alexa:

“Tell them to say ‘Alexa, launch TalesFM,’ and then pick a station using voice or through the app.”

Google Home:

Use the Google Home app to cast TalesFm from a phone.
